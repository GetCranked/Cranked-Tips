MODEL SHIPS - TRACK GUIDE

These tips were compiled from community discussion and insights on Discord.

Major Speed & Line Strategies
  • The "Wood Manny" Gap: The primary challenge and biggest time-saver on the trail is clearing a large gap to a wooden "manual" pad. Use a pre-hop instead of a scrub for a more consistent gap. It's easier from the left side of the lip, but requires you to angle the bike slightly to the right to land on the wood pad.
  • Alternative Wood Manny Line: If you can't make the gap, you can "huck" your bike into the backside of the second lip or the landing itself as a slower, safer option.
  • "Grass Cut" Shortcut: A new, game-changing line was discovered. There is no track marker in a patch of grass, allowing for a direct and significantly faster shortcut. This line is essential for achieving the fastest possible times (sub-39 seconds).
  • End Section Gaps: The final section contains several large gaps you can hit to maintain speed all the way to the finish line. Don't slow down after the wood bridges; look for opportunities to send it to maximize speed.
Section-by-Section Tips
  • S1 (First Split): There is a significant amount of time to be gained in the first section. Look for gaps and lines over rocks to shave off seconds.
  • Rock Garden / S2: This is a critical section for maintaining speed. You must exit the rock garden with high momentum to set up for the major gap. A key technique is to pre-hop the drop immediately after the rock section to carry the ~80 speed needed for the wood manny gap.
  • Transition between S2 & S3: This section is notoriously difficult and is where many players lose time. Focus on executing the pre-hop and gap correctly to set yourself up for the fast end section.
Physics & Game Mechanics
  • Hops are Overpowered: The current bunny hop ("hop") mechanic is considered too powerful. It launches the player very far at high speeds, making the bike feel "arcadey." The developers have acknowledged this and are working on a nerf.
  • Scrubs are Being Developed: The developers confirmed that they are actively working on implementing a proper "scrub" mechanic, which will likely change the high-level meta once released.
  • Cornering vs. Hops: Currently, players with perfect hops are faster than those with perfect cornering, highlighting how dominant the hop mechanic is for speed.
